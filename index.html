Birth of a king

By, Maurice Brown

Forefathers young and old
Bear witness to the birth of a king
Sing his name with praise and jubilation
So that even in the swords in Valhalla look down
In awe for a new warrior destined to join their ranks
May our voices carry to the throne of the All Father
So he may grant our king his wisdom
May the other Gods come to give their blessing
May Thor grant him strength
May Tyr steady his sword hand
And may Freyja Calm his heart.
